"Egyptian multiplication" aka the "Russian Peasant Algorithm"

\begin{eqnarray*} 4a &=& ((a + a) + a) + a \\ &=& (a + a) + (a + a) \end{eqnarray*}

The law of associativity of addition:

\[ a + (b + c) = (a + b) + c \]

Power of 2 1-bits Doublings
1 \(\checkmark\) 59
2 118
4 236
8 \(\checkmark\) 472
16 944
32 \(\checkmark\) 1888

\[ 41 \times 59 = (1 \times 59) + (8 \times 59) + (32 \times 59) \]

Efficiency: \(\mathcal{O}(\log_2 n)\)

\begin{eqnarray*} \#_+(n) &=& \lfloor \log_2 n \rfloor + (v(n) - 1) \\ v(n) &=& \text{the number of 1s in the binary representation of $n$, i.e. the }\textit{population count} \end{eqnarray*}
